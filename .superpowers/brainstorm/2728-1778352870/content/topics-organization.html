How do you want to organize interview topics?

This is the core of the app — tracking preparedness across topics

A

Flat Topic List

Simple list of topics with a preparedness score (0-100%). Add topics freely. Sort by name, score, or last studied. Quick to manage — no hierarchy. Good for broad, varied interview prep.

B

Grouped by Category

Topics organized into categories like "Data Structures", "System Design", "Behavioral", "Domain Knowledge". Each category collapses/expands. Gives structure without being a deep tree. Best for most users.

C

Nested Hierarchy

Unlimited nesting: Category → Sub-topic → Sub-sub-topic. Each level can have its own preparedness score, rolled up to the parent. Most flexible but most complex. Overkill unless you have very deep subjects.
